Disability

Unfinished Business strives to provide equal opportunity to qualified people with disablities. To help measure this, we ask you to tell us if you have a disability or not. This form is completely voluntary. Any answer you give will be kept private and will not be used against you in any way.

Disabilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Blindness
  • Deafness
  • Cancer
  • Diabetes
  • Epilepsy
  • Autism
  • Cerebral palsy
  • HIV/AIDS
  • Schizophrenia
  • Muscular dystropy
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Major depression
  • Multiple sclerosis (MS)
  • Missing limbs or partially missing limbs
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
